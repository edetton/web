The Power of Daily Scripture Study

I am eternally grateful for the scriptures, especially the Book of Mormon. For a period of two weeks in my freshman year, during Covid-19, I committed to read a little bit of the Book of Mormon every night before I went to bed, no matter what. For the first time in my life, I followed through with this, and everything changed. I had pure joy, even though none of the circumstances of my life had changed. I know the Book of Mormon is truly the word of God.
